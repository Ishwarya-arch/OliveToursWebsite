Who We Are!

Olive Tours and Travels Pvt Ltd is a travel agency owned and operated by Joseph Rathinaraj, Anburajan Raj Nayagam and Devasahayam Maruthanayagam.


The mission of our non-profit pilgrimage tours agency is to provide meaningful and enriching experiences to individuals and groups seeking an authentic spiritual journey. We strive to create an environment of support and understanding, while providing guidance and safe passage to sacred locations around the world. Our goal is to facilitate meaningful connections between travelers and the places they visit, and to ensure that these experiences remain meaningful and life-changing even after the journey is complete.
